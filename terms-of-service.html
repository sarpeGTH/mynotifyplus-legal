What the app does

Delivery is best-effort

Notifications are delivered by Apple's iOS notification system, which operates on a best-effort basis. Delivery timing can be affected by iOS settings and behavior — including Focus modes, Scheduled Summary, Low Power Mode, system limits on the number of scheduled notifications, and background activity. We do not guarantee that notifications will be delivered, or that they will be delivered at the exact time requested. Do not rely on the app for time-critical needs.

Keeping notifications active

Due to iOS limits, some schedules (such as random or high-frequency notifications) are programmed in a rolling window and are refreshed when you open the app. If you do not open the app for an extended period, upcoming notifications may run out until you open it again.
